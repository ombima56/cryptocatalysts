Project Overview

This document outlines the procedures for awarding a road construction contract to a contractor and the payment processes by the national government.

1. Planning and Budgeting

Identify the need for road construction, conduct feasibility studies, and allocate funds in the national budget.

2. Project Design and Approval

Prepare detailed project designs, including environmental impact assessments, and obtain necessary approvals.

3. Tendering Process

Develop tender documents, advertise the tender, and screen potential contractors.

4. Bid Evaluation and Contractor Selection

Evaluate bids based on technical capability, experience, and cost, and select the most suitable contractor.

5. Contract Management and Execution

Conduct a kick-off meeting, mobilize resources, and supervise the construction.

6. Payment Process

Payments are made in installments based on project milestones. Each payment is verified and documented.

7. Completion and Handover

Conduct final inspections and hand over the completed road to the relevant authority.

8. Audit and Review

Conduct audits to ensure transparency and document lessons learned for future projects.
